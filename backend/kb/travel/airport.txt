Scenario: At the airport
Role: Airport staff and traveler conversation

Vocabulary:
passport
boarding pass
gate
check-in
luggage
ticket
security
departure

Useful patterns:
I'd like to check in.
Here is my passport.
Where is gate A12?
How many bags can I check in?
What time does boarding start?

Dialogue samples:
Traveler: Hello, I'd like to check in.
Staff: Sure. May I see your passport and ticket, please?
Traveler: Here is my passport.
Staff: Thank you. Do you have any luggage to check in?
Traveler: Yes, one suitcase.
Staff: Great. Your gate is A12 and boarding starts in thirty minutes.

Rules:
Keep the language clear and practical for travel situations.
