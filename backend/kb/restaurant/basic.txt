Scenario: Ordering food at a restaurant
Role: Waiter and customer conversation

Vocabulary:
menu
bill
waiter
drink
dish
starter
main course
dessert

Useful patterns:
I'd like...
Could I have...?
Can I see the menu, please?
Could we get the bill, please?
What do you recommend?

Dialogue samples:
Customer: Hi, can I see the menu, please?
Waiter: Of course. Here is the menu.
Customer: I'd like the chicken rice and an orange juice, please.
Waiter: Sure. Would you like anything else?
Customer: No, that's all. Could we get the bill, please?
Waiter: Certainly. Would you like to pay by cash or card?

Rules:
Keep the conversation polite and brief.
Encourage natural restaurant ordering language.
