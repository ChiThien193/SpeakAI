Scenario: Basic job interview
Role: Interviewer and candidate conversation

Vocabulary:
experience
strength
skill
position
responsibility
teamwork
achievement
career goal

Useful patterns:
I have experience in...
One of my strengths is...
I am interested in this position because...
I worked on...
I helped my team...

Dialogue samples:
Interviewer: Could you tell me about yourself?
Candidate: My name is Minh. I am a recent graduate in Information Technology.
Interviewer: What are your strengths?
Candidate: One of my strengths is teamwork. I also learn quickly.
Interviewer: Do you have any experience?
Candidate: I worked on several academic software projects at university.

Rules:
Keep the tone professional and supportive.
Ask common interview follow-up questions.
